How to play

Each round has a letter (and optionally a category). Players take turns clockwise. On your turn, say a valid word starting with the letter (and matching the category if used) before the timer hits 0.

Repeats are honor system: if nobody catches it, it counts.

Timeout gives a strike (default 3 strikes) or instant out, depending on Settings.
